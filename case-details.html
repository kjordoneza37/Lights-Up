MISION

Light's up is a company dedicated to the design and manufacture of Smart Helms

In order to provide the population with innovative and effective products as a regular means of road protection, promoting the safe use of the bicycle.

Vision

Light's up will be the company preferred by Colombians for the innovation of the Smart Helms product

Being recognized for the high quality of production. In 5 years, we will position ourselves in the main capitals of the country with the aim of reaching the entire population.

Objectives

To design and manufacture bike helms with technology reliable, in order to reduce the accidents of traffic and improve the quality of life of the cyclists.

Implement protection mechanisms for cyclists when going out on the streets. Guarantee the use of cycling helmets, through intelligent technology. Increase the level of satisfaction of our customers and suppliers through high quality standards in Smart Helms.

Product description

SmartHelm is defined as those helmets whose characteristics consist of integrated technology, either to facilitate mobility or to make the route more entertaining. Light´s up will manufacture SmartHelms , what does ours include?

It consists of the integrated use of a gyroscope that facilitates the timely change of route. We integrate a solar panel, which will effectively keep the batteries, lights and other electronics in proper working order. Use of an Arduino Mini for programming and operating our helmet. Microswitches and 5v and 3000 amp batteries The innovation consists of the integrated use of the solar panel and the gyroscope, thus achieving an efficiency in the user protection process.

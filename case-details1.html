History

Light's Up was born from the idea of 7 students

Light's Up was born from the idea of 7 students from Colegio San José Norte, who understood the need to protect cyclists by implementing new signaling tools. Also, Light's Up noticed that, in the process of creating and designing a helmet, companies mistreat the environment, therefore, for the realization of our helmet, we opted for completely environmentally friendly materials.

Theoretical framework

At the end of the 14th century, competition cyclists wanted to protect their heads. They made helmets with plant materials. Helmets broke when hit, but they were probably the best available at the time.

In subsequent years other helmets were created, but these used to be heavy. They did not offer sufficient protection In 1984 standards were established for making helmets. These made it possible to determine which helmets were suitable and remove those that were not safe from sale . Currently, the tech industry has tried to implement smart helmets with brands like Apple and Xiaomi.

Why Light´s Up?

Our company understands the need for safety in cycling transport, obtaining an infallible product when changing lanes.

We offer an effective alternative to protect a bike-rider, by giving the correct information to another rider at the right time. For this, Light's up has designed a Smart helm, with integrated use of a gyroscope, which will make the helmet more efficient and above all will provide ease and greater safety
